Our Mission

During the unprecendented COVID-19 Outbreak, a few bored highschool sophomores decided to get together and create something bigger than themselves. Finance Shminance was the result of this, dedicated to educating the youth about basic financial premises and spreading knowledge to the community

Finance Shminance is dedicated to providing kids ages 12-18 with the resources necessary to achieve financial literacy
